Write-up notes

With the interesting blob identified as admin_credentials.txt, the next step is to download and view its contents directly from the terminal. The challenge hint suggests using the --file /dev/stdout trick so the blob is printed instead of written to disk.

To do that, we run: az storage blob download --account-name neighborhood2 --container-name public --name admin_credentials.txt --auth-mode login --file /dev/stdout | less. This streams the blob contents to standard output and pipes it into less, letting us safely scroll through the exposed credentials.
